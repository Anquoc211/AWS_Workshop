## Vietnam Cloud Day 2025: Ho Chi Minh City Connect Edition for Builders

**Date & Time:** Thursday, September 18, 2025 | 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM VNT

**Venue:** Amazon Web Services Vietnam Office, 36th Floor, 2 Hai Trieu Street, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City

**Status:** Registration Closed

---

## Overview

Vietnam Cloud Day 2025 brought together builders and enterprise leaders for an immersive AWS experience. The event featured government officials, AWS executives, and industry pioneers discussing the latest cloud innovations and AI strategies. Attendees participated in two parallel tracks: a live telecast with keynotes and an in-person breakout session series.
